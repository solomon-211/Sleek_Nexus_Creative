Life at SNC

Why Work With Us?

We build more than products — we build careers, community, and South Sudan's digital future together.

Meaningful Projects

Every project you work on at SNC has real users, real stakes, and real impact. From e-learning platforms serving thousands of students to fintech apps reaching underserved communities — your work matters here.

Collaborative Team

You'll work alongside engineers, designers, trainers, and strategists who are passionate about technology and South Sudan. We share knowledge, review each other's work, and celebrate wins together.

Real Career Growth

We invest in your development — through mentorship, learning budgets, conference access, and a clear path from junior to senior to leadership. Many of our current leads started as interns here.

Work-Life Balance

We have flexible working hours, remote work options, and a culture that respects your time outside of work. Burnout helps no one — we build sustainably and expect you to rest too.

Competitive Compensation

We offer competitive salaries benchmarked to the local and regional market, plus performance bonuses, project completion incentives, and clear salary review timelines.

Health & Wellbeing

Team members have access to health coverage support, mental wellness resources, and an environment where asking for help is encouraged — not penalized.

Our Culture

We Build Things That Last — Including Careers

At Sleek Nexus Creative, we believe that how you build is just as important as what you build. We operate with honesty, move with purpose, and hold each other to high standards — not because we have to, but because we care about the outcome.

We're a lean team, which means every person's contribution is visible and valued. You won't be a cog in a large machine — you'll be a core contributor whose ideas, skills, and decisions shape what we build next.

And because we're based in South Sudan, every product you help create has the potential to improve how organizations serve communities across the country. That context makes the work feel different — and it should.

No politics, no silos

We have direct communication and flat enough structure that every team member can talk to leadership without going through layers of approval.

Your ideas get heard

We have regular team retrospectives and strategy sessions where every person — from intern to senior — can propose ideas that shape our direction.

Mistakes are learning moments

We don't punish honest mistakes — we examine them, learn from them, and document what to do differently. Psychological safety is not just a buzzword here.

How It Works

Our Hiring Process

Transparent, fast, and respectful of your time. Here's exactly what to expect.

1

Submit Your Application

Fill in the form below or apply via the specific role listing. Include your CV and a short cover note explaining why you're interested.

2

Initial Review

Our team reviews every application within 3–5 business days. We'll contact you by email or phone to let you know the next step.

3

Skills Conversation

A 30–60 minute call or in-person session to discuss your experience, review relevant work or a small task, and see how you think.

4

Final Interview

Meet the team lead and discuss role expectations, compensation, start date, and any questions you have about working at SNC.

5

Offer & Onboarding

Receive your written offer, review it, and sign. We then onboard you with a structured first week so you hit the ground running.
