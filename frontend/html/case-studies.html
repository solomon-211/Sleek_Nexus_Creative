Case Study 01

National Schools E-Learning Platform

Client: Ministry of Education Initiative — Juba, South Sudan

Client Brief

A national education initiative needed a scalable e-learning platform to serve 50+ schools across three states, supporting both online and offline learning for students with limited connectivity.

The Challenge

Unreliable internet in rural areas meant a standard web-only platform would fail 70% of users. Content needed to work offline, sync when online, and be operable on low-cost Android devices.

Our Solution

We built a Progressive Web App with service workers for offline content caching, a lightweight admin dashboard, teacher tools for content upload, and SMS notifications for assignments and grades.

Results

  • ? 300% increase in student enrollment
  • ? 85% platform adoption within 3 months
  • ? 12,000+ active student accounts
  • ? Offline mode used by 68% of rural users
React.js Node.js MongoDB PWA / Service Workers SMS API
Case Study 02

Microfinance Mobile Banking App

Client: Juba Community Savings & Credit Union

Client Brief

A local credit union serving 3,000+ members needed to transition from paper-based transaction records to a secure digital system with a mobile app for member self-service.

The Challenge

Strict security requirements for financial data, limited smartphone literacy among members, and the need to integrate with South Sudan's local mobile money networks (MTN, Zain, Airtel Money).

Our Solution

Built a secure Flutter mobile app with biometric login, transaction history, loan application workflow, and mobile money integration. Delivered a teller-side admin web portal for staff.

Results

  • ? 90 days from brief to launch
  • ? 2,400+ members onboarded in month 1
  • ? 60% reduction in transaction processing time
  • ? Zero security incidents in first 12 months
Flutter Firebase Node.js Mobile Money API PostgreSQL

Case Study 03

NGO Program Management Platform

Client: International Humanitarian NGO — South Sudan Operations

Client Brief

An international NGO managing food security and health programs needed a unified platform to track beneficiaries, program activities, field reports, and donor reporting across five counties.

The Challenge

Fragmented data in 6 different spreadsheets across 5 county offices. Staff needed offline data entry capability. International donors required specific reporting formats and audit trails.

Our Solution

Built a full-stack program management system with role-based access, offline-first mobile data collection, automated donor report generation, real-time dashboards, and GPS field mapping.

Results

  • ? 75% reduction in reporting time
  • ? 15,000+ beneficiaries tracked in real-time
  • ? Donor compliance rate improved to 100%
  • ? Used across 5 counties simultaneously
