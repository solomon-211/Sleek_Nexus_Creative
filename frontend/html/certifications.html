Why It Matters

What a SNC Certificate Means

Our certifications are earned, not given. They signal real-world capability backed by coursework, projects, and a final exam.

Competency-Based

Not just attendance — you must complete all assignments, build a real project, and pass a final assessment to earn the certificate.

Employer-Recognized

SNC certificates are recognized by our partner organizations and employers across South Sudan and East Africa who trust our training standard.

Verifiable Online

Every certificate has a unique ID that employers and clients can verify on our website to confirm authenticity.

How to Earn a Certificate

1

Complete the Course

Attend all sessions and complete weekly assignments with a minimum 80% attendance rate.

2

Build a Project

Complete and submit your capstone project demonstrating the skills from the course.

3

Pass the Final Exam

Achieve a minimum score of 70% on the written or practical final assessment.

4

Receive Your Certificate

Get your signed, verifiable SNC certificate in digital and physical format.
