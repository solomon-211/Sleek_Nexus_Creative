Our Social Mission

Technology as a Tool for Equity

Sleek Nexus Creative was founded with a conviction that technology education should not be reserved for the privileged few. Our community programs run alongside our commercial services — using revenue from client projects to fund free and subsidized training for youth, women, and under-resourced communities.

Since 2019, our programs have reached thousands of young people across South Sudan, many of whom have gone on to find employment, launch small businesses, or pursue further technical education.

Our Community Programs

Each program is designed around the real barriers that prevent South Sudanese youth from accessing quality tech education.

Youth Program

Digital Skills for Youth

A free 6-week intensive program for young people aged 16–25 covering computer basics, internet safety, web browsing, office tools, and an introduction to coding.

Ages 16–25 6 weeks Free
Women in Tech

Women in Technology Program

A subsidized training pathway for women who want to enter the technology sector — covering coding, design, and digital entrepreneurship with peer support and mentorship.

Women only 3 months Subsidized
Schools Outreach

Tech in Schools Initiative

We partner with secondary schools across South Sudan to deliver computer literacy lessons, introduce coding, and equip teachers with digital classroom tools.

Secondary schools Ongoing Free to schools
SME Support

Small Business Digital Bootcamp

A 2-day intensive workshop for small business owners covering social media marketing, creating a basic website, using digital payments, and protecting their data.

Small businesses 2 days Free
