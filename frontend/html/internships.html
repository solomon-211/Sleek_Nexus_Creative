Internship Program

Build real-world skills by working on live projects alongside experienced professionals. Gain a portfolio, a certificate, and a career head-start.

Program Overview

Work on Real Projects. Build a Real Portfolio.

Our 3-month internship program places students and recent graduates on active client projects — not busy-work exercises. You'll write real code, design real interfaces, and contribute directly to products used by real organizations.

Every intern is assigned a senior mentor, included in team stand-ups and reviews, and leaves with portfolio pieces that demonstrate genuine competence to future employers.

3Month Duration
4Tracks Available
100%Real Projects

Internship Tracks

Choose the track that matches your skills and career goals.

Software Development

Build full-stack web applications and APIs using Node.js, React, and MongoDB under the guidance of senior engineers.

UI/UX Design

Design wireframes, prototypes, and production-ready UIs in Figma. Work on real client briefs from discovery to handoff.

Digital Marketing

Manage social media, write content, run campaigns, and analyze performance data for SNC and our partner organizations.

Technical Training

Assist in curriculum development, help run workshops, and support learners enrolled in our education programs.

What You Get

Dedicated Mentor

A senior team member guides you throughout the internship — reviewing your work, answering questions, and helping you grow.

Portfolio Projects

Leave with at least 2 completed projects you can show employers, complete with documentation and a case study write-up.

Completion Certificate

An official SNC Internship Certificate verifying your skills, track, and duration — recognized by our partner employers.

Monthly Stipend

Interns in good standing receive a monthly stipend to help cover transport and basic living costs during the program.

Job Pathway

Top performers are considered for full-time roles at SNC. We also actively connect interns with our employer network.

Professional Network

Work alongside experienced professionals and build relationships with tech leaders, NGOs, and businesses across Juba.
