Who We Are

Our Story

From a small team in Juba to South Sudan's leading digital innovation company — here's how it all began.

The Beginning

Born in Juba, Built for South Sudan

In 2019, a group of young South Sudanese technologists and educators gathered with one shared frustration: brilliant talent in South Sudan was going unrecognized, unequipped, and underserved by the digital economy.

The internet was growing. Mobile phones were everywhere. Organizations across Juba desperately needed websites, apps, and digital systems — but had nowhere local to turn for quality, affordable, and context-aware solutions.

So we built Sleek Nexus Creative — a company that would not only deliver world-class technology solutions, but also train the next generation of South Sudanese developers, designers, and digital professionals.

We started with two laptops, one co-working space, and an unshakeable belief that Africa's youngest nation deserved the best technology.

Our Journey

Five Years of Growth

Every milestone represents a commitment kept to the people of South Sudan.

2019

Founded in Juba

Sleek Nexus Creative is officially registered in South Sudan. First team of 4 co-founders begins operations from a co-working space in Juba's city center.

2020

First 10 Clients

Delivered websites and digital systems for 10 organizations including NGOs, government agencies, and private businesses. Launched our first tech training cohort with 25 students.

2021

E-Learning Platform Launched

Developed and launched an e-learning platform serving schools and training institutions across Central Equatoria. Team grows to 12 full-time staff and contractors.

2022

Mobile Development Division

Established a dedicated mobile app development division. Delivered South Sudan's first locally-built fintech mobile app. Partnerships established with 3 international organizations.

2023

100+ Projects Completed

Surpassed 100 completed digital projects. Opened the SNC Academy — a structured training program with certified courses in web development, mobile apps, UI/UX, and digital marketing.

2024

Regional Expansion

500+ students trained. Expanding services across East Africa. Launching SNC's open-source community platform and digital infrastructure programs for rural organizations in South Sudan.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These aren't words on a wall. They're the principles that guide every project, every hire, and every decision we make.

Local First

We build for South Sudan first. Every solution is designed with the local context in mind — bandwidth, language, culture, and user behavior.

Uncompromising Quality

We never cut corners. Whether it's a small NGO website or a national-level platform, we bring the same discipline and craftsmanship to every engagement.

Education as Impact

Training the next generation is not a side project — it's central to our mission. We believe the best investment in South Sudan is its people.

Honest Partnerships

We treat clients as partners. Transparent pricing, clear timelines, honest communication — always. We don't overpromise and underdeliver.

Innovation Without Borders

Geography does not limit creativity. We bring world-class thinking and solutions to South Sudan, and we export South Sudanese talent to the world.

Community-Centered

We measure success not just in revenue but in jobs created, skills transferred, and communities transformed by access to technology.

Our Promise

We're not just building websites. We're building South Sudan's digital future.

Every student we train is a future job creator. Every platform we build enables a community to access services, share knowledge, and grow. Every app we launch connects people to opportunity.

That responsibility is something we carry proudly — and it drives us to keep pushing, keep building, and keep investing in the talent and technology this nation deserves.
