The Challenge

A leading educational institution needed a scalable online learning platform to reach students across multiple locations. They required video hosting, interactive assessments, progress tracking, and certification management.

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive LMS with video streaming, real-time quizzes, discussion forums, and automated grading. The platform includes a mobile-responsive design and integrates with existing student information systems.

Results

  • 10,000+ active students
  • 95% user satisfaction rate
  • 40% increase in course completion
  • 60% reduction in administrative workload
