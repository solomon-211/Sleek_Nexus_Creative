Sponsor a Student

One sponsorship covers tuition, materials, and mentorship for a talented young person who can't afford tech education. One person. One career changed.

The Problem We're Solving

Talent Is Not the Barrier. Cost Is.

South Sudan has no shortage of bright, motivated young people who want to build careers in technology. The barrier is simple: course fees, devices, and materials are out of reach for most families.

A student who can't afford a $200 training fee is shut out of a pathway that could generate tens of thousands of dollars in lifetime earnings and create digital products that serve their entire community.

Your sponsorship removes that barrier completely. The student enrolls, learns, builds a project, earns a certificate, and enters the job market with real, marketable skills.

A Student Story

"Before SNC, I had no way to pay for any training. My sponsor changed everything. I completed the Web Development course, built an NGO website as my final project, and was offered a part-time job three weeks after graduation."

— Web Development Graduate, Juba 2023

How the Sponsorship Works

A simple, transparent process from your decision to a student's graduation.

01

You Choose a Tier

Select the sponsorship level that fits your budget. Every tier makes a real and complete difference.

02

We Match a Student

We identify a qualified student from our waitlist who meets the criteria — ability, commitment, and financial need.

03

Student Enrolls & Trains

The student joins the next cohort, attends all sessions, completes their project, and earns their certificate.

04

You Receive an Update

Depending on your tier, you'll receive progress reports, photos, and a final outcome update on your student's journey.
