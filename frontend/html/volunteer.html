Volunteer Opportunities

Give your time and skills to advance technology education and digital access across South Sudan.

Make a Difference

Your Skills Can Change Someone's Life

South Sudan has thousands of young people eager to learn tech skills and build careers in the digital economy. What they often lack is access to experienced mentors and educators who've walked the path before them.

Whether you can give two hours a week or twenty, your contribution matters. Volunteers receive an official SNC Volunteer Certificate, community recognition, and the satisfaction of directly impacting lives.

Volunteer Roles

Pick the role that fits your skills and availability.

Tech Instructor

Lead weekend or evening coding sessions for students aged 15–30 in web development, Python, or mobile app development.

Community Outreach

Help us reach more students and organizations by representing SNC at schools, events, and community meetings.

Content Creator

Write blog posts, create social media content, or produce short educational videos to grow our online presence and teach our audience.

Event Organizer

Help plan and run hackathons, tech talks, graduation ceremonies, and community events that bring our learners together.

Student Mentor

Provide one-on-one guidance to enrolled students — helping them with projects, career questions, and staying motivated through challenging coursework.
